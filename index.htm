    History

    In 1994 the Central Division was created in the realignment of the entire Major League. The Royals, Whitesox and the Twins came from the West Division and the Indinas and Brewers came from the East Division. In 1998 with the addition of the Devil Rays, the league was realigned again and the Brewers moved to the NL East and the Tigers moved into the AL Central Division. This is now the current layout of the Division. And what most fans dont know is that the AL Central is the only division where each of its members has won the World Series at least once!
